Stockholm Hotels

Find the perfect accommodation for your Stockholm adventure, from luxury waterfront hotels to charming boutique properties in the heart of the city.

Bank Hotel

Das Bank Hotel is located in Norrmalm, Stockholm's city center . It's super central—very close to the main shopping street, Drottninggatan, the waterfront, and just a short walk from the Old Town (Gamla Stan)..

Hotel Frantz

Hotel Frantz ist ein kleines Boutique-Hotel in the heart of Södermalm , one of Stockholm's trendiest neighborhoods. It's located on a quiet street, yet close enough to the Old Town and major attractions to be easily accessible on foot.

The Sparrow Hotel

Das Sparrow Hotel is a boutique hotel in Östermalm, one of Stockholm's trendiest and most exclusive neighborhoods . It's just a short walk to Stureplan, close to great restaurants, bars, and shops. The hotel is located in two charming 19th-century buildings and offers 87 Zimmer und Junior-Suiten , each with a unique design..
